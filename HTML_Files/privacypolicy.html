My Privacy Policy

I don’t collect any personal data from you directly. However, some third-party services I use, like analytics tools, may collect anonymous data when you visit my site. This helps me improve the website and understand how people are using it.

What Data Gets Collected?

Here’s what third-party services might collect when you visit:

  • Pages visited: The specific pages you view on the site.
  • Time of visit: The date and time you come to the site.
  • Duration of visit: How long you stay on each page.

Why Do I Collect This Data?

The data helps me with a few things, such as:
